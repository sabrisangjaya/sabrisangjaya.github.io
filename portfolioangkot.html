Angkot Malang


Malang is one of largest city in Indonesia. Beside known as education city, Malang is also known as tourist destination. Many tourist and newcomer come to malang everyday to go on vacation or to study. Angkot is one of public transportation in Malang city. this system is designed and build to help people knowing route of the angkot. Using custom google map, i redrawing route from text format into graphical one. So the user can get better route visualization.
